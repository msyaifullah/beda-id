# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Beda.id. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information across all our digital products, including websites, mobile applications, and games under the Beda.id brand.

By using our digital products, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our services.

## Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:

- **Personal Information**: Name, email address, contact details (only when provided by you).

- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**: To enhance user experience and improve our services.
- **Payment Information**: When making purchases, payment details are processed securely by third-party payment providers.

## How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain our services.
- Improve user experience and personalize content.

## Data Sharing and Security
- We do not sell your personal information.

- We implement security measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights and Choices

- You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
